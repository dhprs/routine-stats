Introduction

This demonstration dashboard is based on a small sample of mortality data from two hospitals. The variables for the mortality data presented here are period, age, sex and cause of death based on ICD-11.

The full dashboard will contain all data routinely reported data on a monthly basis from all 28 Hospitals. Each hospital will also have a section on the dashboard for its data. The data are organized under tabs which are explained below.

  • All Secondary Facilities: Refers to all 28 secondary facilities in the public sector including stand-alone Maternal and Child Centres and the Lagos State Accident and Emergency Centre, Toll Gate.

  • General Hospital Complexes: Refers to the 26 hospitals including the Maternal and Child Centres within some of the hospitals.

  • General Hospitals: Refers to the 26 general hospitals excluding the Maternal and Child Centres within some of the hospitals and stand-alone Maternal and Child Care Centres.

  • Maternal and Child Centres: Refers to the 10 Maternal and Child Centres, 8 of which are within general hospitals while 2 are stand-alone.

  • Badagry: Administrative Division with 4 hospitals.

  • Epe: Administrative Division with 4 hospitals.

  • Ikeja: Administrative Division with 8 hospitals.

  • Ikorodu: Administrative Division with 2 hospitals.

  • Lagos Island: Administrative Division with 10 hospitals.

The dashboard is updated monthly based on reported by medical records officers at the general hospitals.

For further information, please contact the Director, Directorate of Hospital Planning, Research and Statistic, Lagos State Health Service Commission. Email: dhprs_hsc@gmail.com
